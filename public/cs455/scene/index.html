Use a gamepad with normal FPS controls to move around the scene. Use the directional pad to turn the car wheels and raise and lower the vengeful parking ghost.
Additional keyboard controls:
WASD to move around
Q&E to rotate camera
R&F to move vertically
1&2 to turn car wheels
3&4 to lower and raise the ghost
Arrow keys to move car (according to world cords.)
